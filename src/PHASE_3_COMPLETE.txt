                            QUICK START (2 MIN)

2. Click "Agregar Conductor"
3. Add test driver: Name, phone, vehicle, color, plate
4. Click Save
5. Repeat 3-4 times
6. Create an order as customer (/menu → /checkout)
7. Go to /drivers → "Asignación de Pedidos" tab
8. Click "Auto" on pending order
9. Watch it assign to closest driver ✨
10. Go to /orders as customer, see driver card, photo, rating, map
